1.1 - Faction Sign Up Eligibility

Factions (including Nonames) must sign up with at least 8 members to be eligible.
The current designated representative of the faction should e-mail the coordinator of the event, Varchild with the following information:
  • All faction members' characters' names.
  • All faction members' characters' ids.
  • All decision makers of the faction.
1.2 - Factionless People

If you do not belong to a faction, you may sign up as a mercenary.
Please email the coordinator of the event, Varchild with the following information:
  • Your character name.
  • Your character id.
1.3 - Factionless People

If you are interested in refereeing the event and are an official GA Uniform, please email Varchild with the following information:
  • Your uniform's name.
  • Your uniform's character id.

Section 3 - Tournament Rules

3.1 - Recruiting

Recruiting List
  • All factions start with 50-75 gold coins randomly determined at the start of the Faction Wars.
  • Each player in the recruitment list is equally evaluated by the panel of referees running the Faction Wars and given a recruiting fee.
  • Players not on the list cannot be recruited!
  • A player can refuse to be recruited if he/she can provide a valid reason, or he/she must accept.
  • Players are only recruited for one single event.
  • There is no limit to the number of recruited players as long as there is sufficient gold.
  • All decisions must be made before the deadline for the week. Decisions made after will not be entertained with reason.
3.2 - During The Faction Wars

  • All factions will play all other factions.
  • If at the end of the event there is a tie among factions, it is resolved through one more matchup between the factions.
  • There may not be more than 10 or less than 4 members per event.
  • If the opposing faction fails to turn up at the specific time, the faction will be reassigned to another faction unplayed yet.
3.4 - Fortress Rejuv

  • All players who have lost a game are eligible to play in both Fortress Rejuv event days (Friday/Saturday).
  • No more than 4 members from a faction are allowed to play in a single Fortress Rejuv event. This is to prevent Fortress Rejuv abuse.
  • The top 4 players in a Fortress Rejuv event will receive prizes at the discretion of the judge.
  • Gold rewards will be given to the top 4 places. 25 gold for first, 20 for second, 15 for third and 10 for fourth.
3.4 - How Points Are Calculated

  • A win (the faction is left standing with at least one player without a loss) is two (2) points.
  • A draw (event time is over and both sides have players without losses) is one (1) point.
  • A loss (the opposing team is the only team with at least one player without a loss) is worth zero (0) points.
  • The faction with the most points win!
3.5 - Cheating

  • A maximum of 5 minutes is allowed in order to start matches after the referee announces them. All players who do not start their match within 5 minutes receive a loss at the discretion of the referee.
  • Any player who disobeys any part of the Rules of Conduct set down by Genetic Anomalies will be dealt with accordingly by the Faction Wars III panel of judges.
  • All game logs are to be saved and sent upon request by the referee.
  • If the opponent is intentionally delaying the game, use an alternative method of communication (instant messagers, another copy of Chron X) to inform the judge.
