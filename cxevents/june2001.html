Regular Events Info

Midnight Madness

Run by KLAW, JMJimmy and a few guest judges, the format is the most newbie friendly one in Chron X. Players utilize only uncommons and commons, which are easy to find. Sponsored by Genetic Anomalies.

Saturday Night Live

KLAW's way of torturing the Chron X community. Formats announced the night before, and focused on deck building under tough, or at least odd, restrictions. In addition to the normal prizes for winning, there is a loser's bracket and a creativity prize each week. Sponsored by KLAW (or someone he finds to foot the bill for him).

Sealed Deck League

Genetic Anomalies' sealed deck tournament. There are 10 events from last season's 8, one for each map except World. At the end of each season, points are tallied and prizes are handed out.

Thursday Night Fights

Shoefly holds his own weekly tournament with Thursday Night Fights. Players form 5-player teams, each taking a different domain and battle it out attempting to qualify. The team which qualifies plays against the winning team from the previous week. Sponsored by Genetic Anomalies.

Sergio's Euro Tourney

Sergio, seeing the lack of Europe-friendly tournaments in the past, started running this tournament for his fellow Europeans. The format is always Europe 40/4. Sponsored by Genetic Anomalies.

Scottyboy's Tourney

Another newbie friendly format, this one emphasizes on 1st Edition uncommons and commons as well as another set changed weekly, with it always being Europe 40/3. Sponsored by Genetic Anomalies.

TCF Tango

This idea of this tournament was provided by a member of The Centauri Foundation, Shogun. Different TCFs will take turns to run the tournament. Names can be deceiving, as this tournament as open to all. Sponsored by Genetic Anomalies.

Pack Light Tournament

Run by one of Chron X's two bit brokers, the format is pretty unique in the way it uses Bit Broker prices as well as card restrictions. Micky has been especially helpful in providing a deck checker to verify the deck. Sponsored by Micky Finn.

Glimpse Of Hell

Single elimination tournaments are always the most popular style in Chron X, and The Wun begs to differ. The tournament is classic Swiss and the format changes weekly. Sponsored by Genetic Anomalies.

Peeps R Good VR Lotto

This is no tournament, so don't get mistaken. Peeps R Good runs the weekly VR Lotto, where cards entered into the lotto go into the prize pool. Peeps makes nothing out of it, running it for fun.

Peeps R Good Speed Trivia

Peeps R Good runs the Speed Trivia before his VR Lotto, sponsoring it himself. May the fastest finger win.

Saturday Mega Prize

Using a variant of Swiss, BroodsteR runs a tournament where prizes are usually pretty big, the formats are pretty unusual too. Sponsored by BroodsteR himself.
